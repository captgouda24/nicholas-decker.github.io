Good data is the lifeblood of economists.

Consumer Data

  • Nielsen Homescan - Purchase data down to the bar code level in both a consumer panel and retail panel
  • Kantar World Panel/Numerator - Nielsen Homescan but international
  • Consumer Expenditure Survey - Quantities consumed by households
  • Dominick's Fine Foods - Free but old data at the bar code level
  • Nutritionix - Comprehensive nutritional information

Labor & Employment

  • Automatic Data Processing - Payroll processing company which provides employment data
  • Homebase - Payroll data
  • Revelio - LinkedIn data
  • O*NET - Job tasks
  • Lightcast - Labor market data
  • China Labor Bulletin - All strikes and workplace disputes in China

Manufacturing & Industry

  • Census of Manufactures - Census of all manufacturing firms in America
  • Census of Manufactures (Great Depression) - 1929, 1931, 1933, and 1935
  • Annual Survey of Manufactures - Sample based derivative of the CoM
  • BEA Input-Output Accounts Data - Inputs and outputs by industry

Energy & Environment

  • Oil Price Information Services - Gas station by gas station price information
  • Short-Term Energy Outlook - Oil and gas prices
  • U.S. Energy Information Administration - Energy consumption and prices
  • University of Delaware Terrestrial Precipitation - Gridcell rainfall across the globe
  • Mapbiomass Brasil - Deforestation data
  • CMIP5 - Climate change projections
  • Terraweather - Offshore weather conditions

Trade & International

  • Longitudinal Foreign Trade Transaction Database - Universe of trade data from importers and exporters
  • Harmonized Tariff Schedule of the United States (HSTUS) - Complete listing of products tariff codes

Agriculture & Marine

  • PPM-IBGE - Brazilian Livestock
  • RAM Stock Assessment - Fishery stock assessments
  • Aqua Map - Marine maps
  • Bio-ORACLE - Marine climate

Media & Politics

  • Wisconsin Advertising Project - All political ads up to 2008
  • Wesleyan Media Project - Political ads after 2008
  • TVArchive - Text archives of all television programs
  • Lexis Nexis News Archives - Comprehensive news archives
  • Fourthwall - TV and media data
  • United States Newspaper Panel - Newspaper corpus 1869-2004
  • Nielsen AdIntel - Nielsen ratings and advertising intelligence

Political & Electoral

  • CQ Voting and Elections Collection - Comprehensive electoral data
  • CCES - Voting data from Harvard

Financial & Economic

  • BFI GDP data - Subnational GDP worldwide
  • Wharton Research Data Services - Detailed financial data from many sources
  • Product Sales Database - UK mortgages

Health

  • Massachusetts All Payer Claims Data - All healthcare spending in Massachusetts

Historical & Social

  • Slave Voyages - All recorded slave ships
